# Elevator Pitch

MemeScope AI is a fast research copilot for meme tokens and internet-native projects. Paste a token name, ticker, contract, website, or handle, and it turns that messy signal into a structured report with an executive summary, bull vs bear case, risk flags, meme-angle analysis, diligence checklist, and launch-ready social copy. The key differentiator is that it does both decision support and narrative packaging in one workflow, while explicitly surfacing confidence and missing-data gaps so the output stays trustworthy under noisy market conditions.
